# Code Of Conduct

Be respectful, direct, and constructive.

Do not harass, threaten, or abuse maintainers, contributors, users, or service
operators. Keep discussions focused on Hermes Tweet, Hermes Agent, Xquik, and
the technical work needed to improve the project.

Maintainers may hide, edit, or remove content that is abusive, spammy,
irrelevant, or unsafe.
